Walk-A-Thon Fundraiser

One step at a time ‐ you support your school with every move!

In a Walk-A-Thon, participants walk to raise money for their school or organization. This fun, inclusive event engages students, families, and the community while promoting walking and physical activity. Participants collect flat donations from friends, family, and local supporters, focusing on participation over competition. This delivers a relaxing and community-centered event for kids of all ages.
Walk-a-Thon participants walk for a defined time, usually thirty minutes. Since Walk-a-Thons collect fixed pledges instead of tracking distance or laps, they reduce the need for volunteers. Families watch or join, making the Walk-a-Thon an inclusive and memorable experience for all!
Walk-A-Thon fundraisers benefit both schools and students (participants). They promote a healthy and active lifestyle while offering an effective and engaging way for kids and schools to raise money. They build community and increase awareness for schools' essential causes while remaining flexible and fun ways to meet the specific goals of the school's needs.

Boosts revenue Walk-A-Thons raise considerable funds with minimal effort and expense. Kids, schools, and organizations collect pledges from family and friends, often seeing substantial returns.

Raises money easier PledgeStar makes it easy for participants to set up personal fundraising pages that collect donations from a vast network of family and friends. It also tracks pledges in real-time, so you're always up-to-date.

Costs less Walk-a-Thons raise funds efficiently and effectively with minimal expenses like event setup and promotional materials. Most of the funds go directly to the cause. Plus, with PledgeStar, the more you raise, the less you pay. We charge only 7% of the donations but never more than $1,195. This means that as you raise more, the percentage of the earnings you keep increases.

Engages the community Walk-A-Thons unite the entire community, from students and parents to neighborhood supporters. By involving the wider community, schools build stronger relationships and increase support for their current and future initiatives.

Easy for admins PledgeStar simplifies managing Walk-A-Thon fundraisers by automating tasks like donation collection and partisan incentive tracking. This allows organizers to focus on creating an enjoyable experience. With easy-to-use reporting, PledgeStar reduces the admin workload, making the event successful and stress-free.

Flexible PledgeStar offers flexibility, adapting to your school or organization's needs. Whether you plan a small event with a few participants or a large-scale community walk, PledgeStar's customization options match your theme and fundraising goals.
Plan your Walk-A-Thon and ensure it goes smoothly with a well-structured and detailed timeline and schedule. PledgeStar helps you define the steps leading up to your event, from setting fundraising goals to event promotion and donation tracking.

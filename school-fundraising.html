Why Fundraise with PledgeStar

RAISE MORE

PledgeStar helps students raise $175 on average - more than any other platform! How? Easy, fun outreach through automated emails, texts, and social media, and proprietary money raising features no other system offers.

SPEND LESS

Keep more of what you raise! Pay PledgeStar just 7% of the amount raised up to a maximum of $1,195. No hidden fees, no surprises - just more money for your school!

STRESS-FREE AND EFFORTLESS

Set it up, sit back, and watch donations roll in! Your families enter names and contact information, and within minutes, our platform sends emails, texts, and social media posts - effortless fundraising with no stress!

REACH EVERY FAMILY, NO MATTER THE LANGUAGE

WE'VE GOT YOUR BACK
